the amniotic tranquility of being indoors during a thunderstorm

❮ ❯


It has been a long, exhausting day. As bedtime approaches, you slip into your most comfortable pajamas and get underneath the soft, fluffy, warm covers of your bed. You draw the curtains and light a candle, reveling in the peace and tranquility of your bedroom. As your mind and body begin to relax, you begin to hear raindrops dancing off the rooftop over your head. Slow rolls of thunder soon follow, and every now and then you can hear an occasional crack of lightning. The cadence of the raindrops slowly start to lull you to sleep. You drift off in a dreamlike state, cocooned in your bed and serenaded by the symphony of the storm outside.
